Peninsula Party Delivers Happiness!

Founded in 2005 by Feliciano (Chano) and Ines Zavala with the vision of providing the Bay Area with a new and efficient way to deliver party rentals. We are the Bay Area’s source for discount party rentals and Balloons. Headquartered in Palo Alto CA, we service the Peninsula, South Bay and San Francisco Area.

We provide top quality party rentals and balloons for birthday parties, corporate events, church and non-profit events at the best prices possible.

Our friendly staff is always available to answer any questions.
